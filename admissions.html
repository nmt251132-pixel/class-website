Admissions

We welcome new students each year. Here’s how to apply:

  1. You can get application form in Mayu Foundation Office or Mayu Refional Centre-MRC
  2. If you don't download the admission form from our website.
  3. Fill out the form and submit it to the school office.
  4. Attend the entrance interview if required.
  5. Check the results on our website or notice board.
